Nikaah : Post Namaz-e-Asr

The Nikah is the official marriage ceremony in Islam. It includes the recitation of the sermon and the marriage contract in front of witnesses, the exchange of vows, and the offering of the Mehr (gift) from the groom to the bride. Prayers and blessings are recited for the couple's future together.
